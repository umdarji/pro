Welcome to our state-of-the-art Bus Management System (BMS), a comprehensive digital solution revolutionizing public transportation management. This advanced system integrates cutting-edge technology with practical transportation needs to create a seamless experience for both operators and passengers. Our BMS addresses the complex challenges of modern urban mobility while ensuring efficiency, safety, and passenger satisfaction.

Designed with scalability in mind, our system caters to transportation networks of all sizes - from small local operators to large metropolitan transit authorities. It combines real-time GPS tracking, smart scheduling algorithms, automated booking systems, and comprehensive fleet management tools in one integrated platform. The system's modular architecture allows for easy customization to meet specific operational requirements while maintaining robust performance and reliability.

System Access and User Roles

The system supports multiple user roles with different access levels and capabilities:

1. Administrators: Full access to all system features, including route management, staff scheduling, and financial reporting.
2. Staff: Access to daily operations like ticket booking, passenger management, and bus scheduling.
3. Drivers: Access to route details, passenger lists, and vehicle maintenance records.
4. Passengers: Access to booking system, travel history, and personal profile management.
